Panes de Yuca

Description

Delicious Ecuadorian cheesy dough bites that are sure to be your new obscession.

  • 2.5 cups yuca flour
  • 4 cups grated queso blanco
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Combine the yuca flour, backing powder, and salt
  3. Add the cheese and combine well
  4. Add the butter and eggs and mix together until it forms a ball (it will be wet but shouldn't stick to your fingers)
  5. Separate into bite-sized balls and place on a cooking sheet.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown
