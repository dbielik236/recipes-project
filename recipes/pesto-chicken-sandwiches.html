Pesto Chicken Sandwiches

Description

Who doesn't love mayonaise, pesto, and freshly baked bread?! Well this recipe has all three!

Ingredients

  • Fresh baked bread (don't get the cheap stuff!)
  • 1 pound chicken (cooked and pulled)
  • 1 cup mayonaise
  • 1 cup pesto
  • Roma tomatoes
  • Arugula
  • Mozzarella, sliced
  • Salt/pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Mix chicken, mayo, pesto, and salt/pepper in a bowl. Evenly coat and set aside in the fridge
  2. Spread mayonaise on one side of the bread slices and grill until crispy
  3. Slice the tomoatoe very thinly
  4. On the bread, layer the chicken, tomoatoes, sliced mozzarella, and arugula
  5. Slice the sandwich in half and devour!
